ABOUT US

At Mindful Minds Psychiatry, we are committed to offering personalized and comprehensive mental health services. We prioritize creating a safe and supportive environment where individuals can feel heard, understood, and empowered to improve their mental wellness.

About us

Consultation Liaison Services

PinkCity Psychiatry offers specialized Consultation Liaison Psychiatry services, bridging the gap between mental and physical health. Our team, led by Dr. Kashish Thaper, collaborates closely with medical specialists to address the interplay between mind and body in managing health challenges. Dr. Thaper is the first CL Specialist in Jaipur.

Comorbid Substance Use Management

Our team specializes in addressing the complex relationship between substance use disorders and medical conditions. We offer personalized treatment plans that integrate psychiatric and medical care to support individuals in overcoming addiction while managing their physical health challenges effectively.

Medical Treatment Compliance

We understand the importance of adhering to medical treatments for optimal health outcomes. Our specialists work closely with patients to identify and address barriers to treatment adherence, providing support and strategies to ensure compliance with prescribed medications and therapies.

Decision Making Support

Making decisions about medical treatments can be overwhelming, especially when facing complex health issues. Our team provides expert guidance and support to patients and their families, empowering them to make informed decisions about their healthcare options based on their individual needs and preferences.

Comorbid/Multimorbid Psychiatric Concerns

Many individuals with medical illnesses also experience psychiatric symptoms or disorders. Our specialists are trained to recognize and address these comorbidities, offering integrated treatment plans that consider both physical and mental health aspects to optimize overall well-being.

Illness-related Stress Management

Coping with a medical diagnosis or chronic illness can be challenging and may exacerbate existing mental health concerns. We provide personalized support and coping strategies to help individuals manage stress related to their medical conditions, promoting resilience and emotional well-being.

Medication Management

Collaborating closely with medical specialists, we ensure that psychiatric medications are prescribed judiciously and in consideration of other medications being taken. Our goal is to minimize polypharmacy and potential drug interactions while optimizing treatment outcomes for our patients.
