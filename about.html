Frequently asked questions

How long do appointments typically last?

Appointment durations vary depending on the nature of the visit and the services being provided. Initial evaluations may last longer, typically around 60-90 minutes, while follow-up appointments are usually shorter, averaging 30-60 minutes.

What payment options are available for services rendered?

We accept various payment methods, including cash, credit/debit cards, and electronic funds transfer. Additionally, we offer flexible payment plans and financing options for those who qualify. Please inquire with our billing department for more information.

Can I refill my prescriptions online?

Yes, we offer convenient online prescription refill services through our patient portal. Simply log in to your account, submit a refill request, and our team will process your request promptly. If you have any questions or concerns about your medications, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Are telehealth appointments available for remote consultations?

Yes, we offer telehealth appointments for both new and existing patients. Telehealth allows you to consult with our providers from the comfort of your own home using secure video conferencing technology. This convenient option is ideal for individuals who are unable to visit our office in person.

What languages are spoken by your staff?

Our staff is proficient in multiple languages to accommodate the diverse needs of our patients. While English is the primary language of communication, we also have staff members who are fluent in Hindi, Punjabi, and other regional languages. If you require language assistance, please inform us when scheduling your appointment.
